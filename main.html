Mecca (Makkah)

Mecca the holiest city in islam, is home of several iconic landmarks.

  1. The Great Mosque of Mecca (Masjid al-Haram), housing the Kaaba, the most sacred site.
  2. The Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad), embedded in one corner of the Kaaba.
  3. Jabal Al Nour, the mountain where the Prophet received the first revelation.
  4. The Abraj Al-Bait Towers, which include the Clock Tower and luxury hotels.
  5. The Maqaam-e-ibrahim, is a small square stone associated with Ibrahim

Must-Try Meccan Foods:

  • Sambousek: Fried stuffed pastries filled with savory goodness.
  • Al-Mandi: Barbecued lamb served with fragrant rice.
  • Shawarma: Flavorful meat wraps with tahini sauce.
  • Soubiya: A traditional drink made from grains, sugar, milk, and cinnamon.
  • Souiyka: Delicious desserts made with Ajwa dates and grain flour.
